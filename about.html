Welcome to the About page of Amarkantak Tourism website!

Amarkantak, located in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh, is a beautiful hill town that is considered to be the source of the holy rivers Narmada, Son, and Johila. It is a place of immense natural beauty, religious significance, and historical importance. It attracts tourists from all over the world who come here to explore the various sights and experiences that it has to offer.

Amarkantak Tourism website is dedicated to providing visitors with all the information they need to plan their trip to this incredible destination. Whether you are interested in exploring the natural beauty of the place, learning about its rich cultural heritage, or simply enjoying some peace and quiet, we have got you covered.

Our website features detailed information about all the major tourist attractions in Amarkantak, including the various temples, waterfalls, and viewpoints that make this place so special. We have also included information about the various activities and experiences that visitors can enjoy here, such as trekking, bird watching, and more.

In addition to this, we have also included information about the various accommodations and restaurants available in Amarkantak, to help visitors plan their stay here. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses, we have something for everyone.

At Amarkantak Tourism, we are committed to providing our visitors with a memorable experience. We believe that everyone who visits this beautiful place should leave feeling enriched, inspired, and rejuvenated. We hope that our website will be your go-to resource for all things Amarkantak, and that you will come to love this place as much as we do.


Thank you for choosing Amarkantak Tourism website, and we look forward to welcoming you to this incredible destination soon!
